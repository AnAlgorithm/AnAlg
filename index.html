Rubik's cubes

Welcome to ALL ABOUT RUBIK'S CUBES. The Rubik's cube was invented in 1974 by Hungarian sculpturer and professor of architecture Erno Rubik. Originally called the Magic Cube, the puzzle was licensed by Rubik to be sold by Ideal Toy Corp. in 1980 via businessman Tibor Laczi and Seven Towns founder Tom Kremer.
